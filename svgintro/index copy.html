Episode I

THE STORM


The Shitstorm has arrived. Admiral Akuban has assembled all able bodied troops to collect poop by any means neccessary, since evaporational poop vapour is the only current source of fresh water available left to Earth. The poop must be pure in order to be properly recycled and is not to include any garbage being dumped by the Zurks...

Rabid animals, discarded underwear and other debris will be close to impossible to evade.
